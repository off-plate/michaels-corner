## Sound treatment
- Hard floors + bare walls = echo. Add: rug, curtain, soft furniture in frame area.
- AC / fridge / street noise audit: do a 30s silent record and listen back

## Visual consistency rules
- Same background = recognizable brand (good for long-form series)
- Different background per platform = OK if intentional (e.g. handheld for Shorts)
- Wardrobe: 2–3 staple outfits that work with your background
