## Typography
- **Display / thumbnails**: [font name] — bold, high contrast
- **Body / captions**: [font name] — readable at small size
- **Never use**: default platform fonts (signals low effort)

## Logo / wordmark
- File: `brand/assets/` (add files when finalized)
- Safe zone: equivalent to height of the "M" around all sides
- Don't stretch, recolor, or add effects

## Thumbnail style rules
- Max 3 visual elements: face, object, text
- Text: 3–4 words MAX, one font weight
- Readable at 120px width — zoom out to test
- Face: clear expression, eye contact, no neutral faces

## Voice & tone
- Direct, no padding
- Slightly warm, not corporate
- Czech: conversational, not formal
- English: B2 level — clear, no idioms, no slang you're not sure about
- Never: clickbait that doesn't deliver, fake urgency, "you won't believe" energy

## What Michael's Corner is NOT
- A generic productivity / lifestyle channel
- A corporate consulting brand (that's Off-Plate)
- A finance-bro channel
