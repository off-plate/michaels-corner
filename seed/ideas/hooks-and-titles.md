# Hooks & Title Formulas

Recyclable patterns. Steal from yourself.

## Hook patterns (first line)
- "Most people [X]. Here's why that's wrong."
- "I tried [X] for [time]. Here's what happened."
- "If you're [audience], stop doing [common thing]."
- "Three things I wish I knew about [X] before I started."
- "The reason [common belief] is actually a lie."
- "I spent [money/time] so you don't have to."

## Title formulas (English)
- `How I [outcome] in [timeframe]`
- `Why [common belief] is wrong`
- `[Number] [things] every [audience] should know`
- `The truth about [topic]`
- `I tried [X]. Here's what happened.`
- `Stop doing [X]. Do this instead.`

## Title formulas (Czech)
- `Jak jsem [outcome] za [časový rámec]`
- `Proč [běžný názor] nefunguje`
- `[Číslo] věcí, které by měl každý [cílovka] vědět`
- `Pravda o [téma]`
- `Zkusil jsem [X]. Tady je výsledek.`
